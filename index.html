EPISODES

S01 Season 1

Tommy Shelby, a dangerous man, leads the Peaky Blinders, a gang based in Birmingham. Soon, Chester Campbell, an inspector, decides to nab him and put an end to the criminal activities.

E01 Episode 1

12 Sept 2013

Birmingham gangster Thomas Shelby sees an opportunity to move up in the world when a crate of guns goes missing.

E02 Episode 2

19 Sept 2013

Thomas Shelby provokes the ire of local kingpin Billy Kimber by fixing a horse race and Inspector Campbell carries out a vicious raid of Small Heath in search of the stolen guns.

E03 Episode 3

26 Sept 2013

Thomas Shelby takes Grace to Cheltenham races in a bid to get closer to Billy Kimber, and IRA sympathisers make an offer to buy the stolen guns.

E04 Episode 4

03 Oct 2013

The war with the gypsies escalates, Thomas is put under pressure to deliver the stolen guns, and Thomas's brother John plans to marry a former prostitute, but Thomas suspects that she's still on the game.

E05 Episode 5

10 Oct 2013

Thomas deals with an IRA chief looking to avenge his cousin's death. Campbell gets closer to the stolen guns, and Grace has to decide where her loyalties lie.

E06 Episode 6

17 Oct 2013

As Thomas prepares to oust Billy Kimber, hidden family secrets are revealed, and Campbell unleashes one last plan to destroy the gang.

Best Peaky Blinders Moments Season 1

The story began with a backdrop of Birmingham 1991 following the events of the First World War. It introduces the character of Tommy Shelby who gets a consignment of guns. On the other hand, Inspector Campbell tries to retrieve them but Shelby decides to keep them for leverage.

The final battle with Kimber | S01E06 | Peaky Blinders.

Billy Kimber: The leader of the Birmingham Boys (also known as the Brummagem Boys) was a real gang leader and possibly the biggest crime boss in England. In real life, he died of a prolonged illness rather than a shot from Thomas Shelby's gun.
